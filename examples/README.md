# Examples

Double-click any of these `.html` files to watch a finished Magic Graphics clip play in your
browser. These are real outputs - the kind of thing you'll get by just talking to Magic Graphics.

- **example-story-beats.html** - a 3-beat story clip that builds to a payoff
- **example-barbershop-hype.html** - bold words slamming in on the beat
- **example-newsletter-stat.html** - a big number counting up with a glow
- **example-dreamy-broll.html** - a flowing background to lay footage or text on top of

Want one for *your* brand? Install the skill (see [../INSTALL.md](../INSTALL.md)) and say
"make me a clip for my video."
