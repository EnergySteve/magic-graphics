# ✨ Magic Graphics

### Animated graphics, no skills needed.

**Make the cool moving-text clips you see in videos - just by talking.**

You know the glowing words, the punchy titles, the dreamy backgrounds that play in people's
Reels and TikToks? Magic Graphics makes those for you. You tell it what your video is about,
like you're talking to a friend, and it hands you a finished vertical clip to post or drop into
your video. No design skills. No editing software. No tech.

> **In one line:** You talk, it makes the moving graphic, you post it.

---

## What you can make

Seven styles, all vertical (phone-shaped), all ready in about a minute:

- **Story clips** - a few words that build to a payoff (great for hooks, before/after, "the result")
- **Big number / stat** - a number that counts up with a glowing climb ("13,000 views")
- **Title slam** - bold words that punch in on the beat
- **Kinetic type** - a quote or line that builds word by word
- **Word burst** - one powerful word that shatters into sparks
- **Handle reveal** - your @name assembling for an intro or outro
- **Dreamy background** - flowing color you lay your footage or text on top of (classic b-roll)

Don't know which you want? Just say what your video is about. Magic Graphics suggests the story,
the look, the words, and the colors. You never face a blank page.

---

## Why it's different

- **You just talk.** No menus, no timelines, no design jargon. It asks simple questions and picks
  great defaults if you're not sure.
- **Zero install, zero cost to run.** The clips are self-contained files that play in any web
  browser. Nothing to download, no accounts, no monthly fees, nothing uploads anywhere.
- **Your brand, instantly.** Tell it one color ("hot pink," "blue") and the whole clip reskins.
  Or pick a ready-made look: **Bold Gold** or **Clean & Premium**.
- **Made for posting.** Every clip is 1080x1920 - the exact shape for Reels, TikTok, and Stories.

## How to get a video out of it

The clips play on a loop in your browser. To post one, you screen-record it (your phone or
computer already does this - takes 10 seconds). Magic Graphics walks you through it step by step.
Want a real video file instead? It can do that too if you're comfortable in a terminal.

## Install it - paste one line into Claude

**No downloads. No setup. Nothing to figure out.** Magic Graphics is a **skill** for Claude
(works in **Claude Code**, or any Claude with Skills). To install it, open Claude and paste
this one line:

> **Set up the Magic Graphics skill for me: download it from https://github.com/EnergySteve/magic-graphics and copy the `magic-graphics` folder (the one with `SKILL.md` inside) into my Claude skills folder at `~/.claude/skills/`. Then make me a quick test clip so I know it's working.**

Claude grabs everything and installs it for you. When it's done, start a new chat and say
*"make me some b-roll for my video."* That's the whole setup.

Want the manual steps instead? See **[INSTALL.md](INSTALL.md)**.
